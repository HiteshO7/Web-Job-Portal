    • Our free trial allows you to explore JobSeek's features and services for a limited time without any cost. During the trial period, you'll have access to basic features such as searching for jobs, creating a profile, and applying to a limited number of job postings. After the trial period ends, you'll have the option to subscribe to a paid plan to continue accessing premium features.

    • At JobSeek, we utilize advanced search filters and algorithms to help you find job listings that match your specific criteria. You can customize your search by factors such as location, industry, job type, salary range, and required qualifications. Our goal is to streamline the job search process and connect you with opportunities that align with your preferences and skills.

    • When evaluating a founding team, we look for a combination of skills, experience, and passion. A strong founding team typically consists of individuals with complementary skill sets, a deep understanding of the industry, and a shared vision for the company's growth. We also value traits such as creativity, resilience, and the ability to adapt to challenges. Ultimately, we seek teams that demonstrate the potential to execute their business plan effectively and drive long-term success.

    • The choice between Pay-as-you-go (Paa) and Prepay depends on your preferences and usage patterns. Pay-as-you-go allows you to pay for services as you use them, offering flexibility and scalability. Prepay, on the other hand, involves paying upfront for a set amount of service, often at a discounted rate. If you anticipate using JobSeek frequently and want to save money in the long run, prepaying may be a better option. However, if you prefer to pay only for what you use, Pay-as-you-go might be more suitable.

    • With our free plan, you can access basic features and services on JobSeek at no cost. While the free plan has some limitations compared to our paid plans, it still provides valuable resources to aid you in your job search journey.
